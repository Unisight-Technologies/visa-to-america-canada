PROVNCIAL NOMINEE PROGRAM

A Canadian province or territory has to nominate you to immigrate to Canada through the Provincial Nominee Program (PNP).




THE PROVINCIAL NOMINEE PROGRAM IS FOR APPLICANTS WHO:


  • Have the skills, education and work experience to contribute to the economy of a specific province or territory

  • Want to live in that province

  • Want to become permanent residents of Canada.

PROVINCIAL NOMINEE PROGRAM PROCESSING CRITERIA


Processing is similar to Express Entry with the additional steps of:



1. Creating a client profile in the particular province where the skills are in-demand

2. Client requires to pay the province PNP fees, which varies according to province.

Completing an online PNP profile or entering the pool does not guarantee that province will invite client to apply for permanent residence (PR).
