INTAKE

months only:

TIME FRAME

Time frames for processing:

  • • 3 -6 months for processing
  • • 2 – 3 months for letter of acceptance
  • • 1 – 2 months for study permit





ELIGIBLE FOR

  • • 3 years of Post Graduate Work Permit
  • • Create profile in Express Entry, Canadian Experience Class after one-year Canadian work experience
  • • Based on the comprehensive ranking score (CRS) eligible to receive an invitation to apply (ITA) for permanent residence (PR)
  • • Submit application for PR


STUDENT PROCESSING CRITERIA

Initial Intake

1. Two-year diploma program.

2. Forward all required documentation: Resume, passport copy, high school certificate, grade 10/12, marks above 60 or 70, etc.

3. IELTS academic – 6.0 in listening, reading, writing, speaking.

4. Proof of funds.

No scholarship, No PhD, No Master’s.

Fees

Towards communications: VisaTocanada (VTC) provides student’s profile for assessment. JM Immigration assesses student’s profiles, college search, completion of application forms, submission to college, communication with college

1. CAD $250 (at the start of process, non-refundable).

2. College application fees varies between colleges: CAD $100 to 150, paid by student.

3. College acceptance depends on the number of students.

College or University

JM Immigration will recommend one institution.


Time Frame

1. From the time student documents received: 2 to 4 months for Letter of Acceptance (LOA).

2. Student is kept updated when we receive communication from college.

3. 5 months ideal time for processing.

4. Time required for processing Study Permit application from IRCC.

Student Permit fees

1. CAD $1,500 – Our professional fees for preparing application submission to IRCC.

2. Student Permit fees from IRCC – CAD $ 150.00, student pays, provides us with Credit Card details for processing.

Commission structure

1. Step A: VTC receives 10% of Student Permit fees after study permit is approved by IRCC.

2. Step B: JM Immigration:
a) CAD $ 250 wire transfer to start the process
b) CAD $ 1,500 for Student Permit.

Retainer Agreement

1. Student requires to sign the Retainer Agreement.

College Commission

1. Commission that is received from the college is 50% for VTC and 50% for JM Immigration.

2. Commission is received once student starts the semester in college and does not drop out or change college after arrival in Canada.

3. If the student changes college during the first year, no commission from college.
